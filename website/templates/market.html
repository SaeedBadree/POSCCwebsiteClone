Markets in Port of Spain

The Port of Spain City Corporation manages several markets across the city, providing essential spaces for local vendors and ensuring the availability of fresh produce to the community.

Markets Managed by the Port of Spain Corporation

Central Market

Located in the heart of Port of Spain, the Central Market offers a variety of fresh produce, meats, and fish. It is the largest market in the city and caters to thousands of residents daily.

Location: 1 Market Street, Port of Spain

Opening Hours: Monday - Saturday: 6 AM - 6 PM

St. James Market

Known for its vibrant atmosphere, St. James Market is a hub for local vendors selling fresh fruits, vegetables, and other local goods. It is particularly busy during the weekends.

Location: 10 Western Main Road, St. James

Opening Hours: Monday - Sunday: 7 AM - 5 PM

Woodbrook Market

Located in the bustling district of Woodbrook, this market offers an assortment of fresh seafood and locally sourced produce.

Location: 45 Tragarete Road, Woodbrook

Opening Hours: Tuesday - Sunday: 8 AM - 4 PM
