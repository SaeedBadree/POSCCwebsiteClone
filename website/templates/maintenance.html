Maintenance Services

The Port of Spain City Corporation provides essential maintenance services to ensure the well-being and safety of our city. Our team is dedicated to maintaining roads, sanitation, and the natural environment.

Maintenance Work We Provide

Our maintenance services include:

  • Road Repair Services
  • Sanitation Services
  • Tree Cutting Services

We are responsible for maintaining and repairing roads across the city to ensure safe driving conditions. Our services include:

  • Pothole patching
  • Asphalt resurfacing
  • Sidewalk repairs

To report road issues, Download the road repair request form and submit it to our office.

Our sanitation team works daily to keep the city clean and hygienic. We provide:

  • Street sweeping
  • Garbage collection and disposal
  • Graffiti removal

For more information on waste collection schedules, please contact us at sanitation@poscity.gov.

We maintain trees in public spaces, ensuring the safety of residents by trimming or removing overgrown or hazardous trees. Services include:

  • Tree trimming and pruning
  • Emergency tree removal (after storms)
  • Maintenance of trees in parks and public areas

To request tree cutting services, Download the tree cutting request form and submit it to our office.
