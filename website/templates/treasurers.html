Treasurer's Department

The Treasurer's Department ensures financial stability and regulatory compliance for the city. Our role involves maintaining proper records, reporting financial transactions, payroll services, and managing financial risks for the Corporation.

General Information

The Treasurer's Department consists of approximately 60 employees and operates under the leadership of the Ag. City Treasurer Ms. Leslie-Ann John. The department is divided into seven units to ensure specialized services are efficiently delivered:

  • Treasurer's Department
  • Abstract Unit
  • Payables Unit
  • Payroll Unit
  • Ledgers Unit
  • Check Staff Unit
  • Cashiers Unit
  • Budgets

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Treasurer's Department: Ensures proper infrastructure and human capital for smooth operation. Ensures internal controls are maintained to mitigate errors and provide accurate financial reports.
  • Abstract Unit: Reconciles the Corporation's bank accounts and ensures accurate cash flow management and fraud detection.
  • Payables Unit: Handles administrative expenses, verifies invoices, and prepares payment vouchers for suppliers.
  • Payroll Unit: Manages employee payroll, ensuring statutory deductions and salary disbursements comply with regulations.
  • Ledgers Unit: Records property taxes and maintains records for easy retrieval and calculation.
  • Check Staff Unit: Verifies the accuracy of transactions to reduce the risk of financial statement misstatements.

Services Provided

  • Budgeting: Facilitates ethical budgeting practices, manages income and expenditure, and prepares budgets.
  • Treasury Management: Manages the Corporation's bank accounts, ensures proper cash flow, and prepares monthly reports.
  • Compliance: Ensures regulatory compliance with financial practices, aligning assets with financial records.
  • Record Keeping: Documents income and expenditure, facilitating audits and ensuring transparency.
  • Account Management: Manages relationships with customers and suppliers to foster growth and retention.
  • Risk Analysis: Assesses operational, business, and computer security risks to protect data and ensure smooth operations.
