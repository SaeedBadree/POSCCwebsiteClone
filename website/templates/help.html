Frequently Asked Questions

What are the opening hours of the Port of Spain City Corporation?

The Port of Spain City Corporation is open from 8am to 4pm, Monday to Friday.

How can I apply for a building permit?

You can apply for a building permit through our online portal or by visiting the Building Inspectorate office.

Where can I pay my property taxes?

Property taxes can be paid at the Finance Department or through our online payment system.
