City Police Service

The Port of Spain City Police Service enforces the Municipal Corporation Act 25:04 and all other laws of the Republic of Trinidad and Tobago. The City Police officers work with other enforcement agencies to ensure law and order and the protection and safety of the Port of Spain Corporation’s properties, employees, and burgesses.

Roles and Functions

The roles and functions of the Port of Spain City Police Service include the following:

  • Enforce the Laws enshrined in Act 21 of 1990 and the Port of Spain Corporation Bye Laws.
  • Investigate reports made by members of the public and take appropriate action.
  • Conduct foot and mobile patrol duties.
  • Serve and execute summons and warrants as requested by Magistrates.
  • Perform VIP escort duties for the Mayor and Council members.
  • Enforce traffic laws and control traffic flow.
  • Escort prisoners to and from courts and prisons.
  • Preserve order at political meetings, public functions, and court precincts.
  • Conduct drills, parades, and lectures for supervisors and other personnel.
  • Provide counseling and prosecution for victims and offenders.
  • Assist law enforcement agencies and NGOs with training and support activities.
  • Ensure the safety and security of Corporation properties and personnel.

Departmental Information

The sanctioned strength of the City Police Department is 124 officers, sub-divided as follows:

  • 1 Superintendent of Police
  • 1 Assistant Superintendent of Police
  • 2 Police Inspectors
  • 6 Police Sergeants
  • 12 Police Corporals
  • 102 Police Constables

At present, the actual strength is as follows:

  • 1 Superintendent of Police
  • 1 Assistant Superintendent of Police
  • 2 Police Inspectors
  • 6 Police Sergeants
  • 12 Police Corporals
  • 78 Police Constables

Vacant Positions

  • 1 Superintendent of Police
  • 1 Assistant Superintendent of Police

Task Force Unit

This unit is responsible for serious investigations, executing warrants, performing VIP escorts, and protecting Corporation properties. The unit consists of:

  • 1 Acting Sergeant
  • 7 Police Constables

Welfare Unit

The Welfare unit comprises:

  • 1 Acting Sergeant
  • 3 Police Constables

The Welfare Unit handles walk-in clients, school matters, uncontrollable children, mediation, counseling, and apprehension of sexual offenders.

Divisions and Units

The Port of Spain City Police Service workforce is divided into four (4) general policing units:

  • Unit One – 1 Acting Corporal, 8 Police Constables
  • Unit Two – 1 Acting Corporal, 9 Police Constables
  • Unit Three – 1 Acting Corporal, 9 Police Constables
  • Unit Four – 1 Acting Corporal, 8 Police Constables

Additionally, two (2) Street Patrol Units, comprising:

  • 1 Acting Sergeant
  • 1 Acting Corporal
  • 14 Police Constables

The main function of these units is to patrol the city streets, maintain law and order, preserve peace, and detect crimes.

Motorcycle Unit

The Motorcycle Unit comprises:

  • 1 Acting Corporal
  • 2 Police Constables

Responsibilities include processing duties for management, delivering notices, and providing VIP escort services for the Mayor, Council members, and dignitaries.

Task Force Unit

This unit handles serious investigations, executing warrants, VIP escorts, and ensuring the safety of Corporation properties and personnel. The unit consists of:

  • 1 Acting Sergeant
  • 7 Police Constables

Court and Process

This section is responsible for civil and criminal court matters, escorting prisoners, and preserving order in court precincts. The unit consists of:

  • 1 Acting Assistant Superintendent
  • 1 Inspector
  • 1 Acting Sergeant
  • 1 Police Constable (Court Orderly)
