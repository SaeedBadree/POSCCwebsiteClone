Property Management Department

The Property Management Department oversees the properties owned by the Port of Spain Corporation for legal and illegal occupation through Lease Agreements, encompassing residential, commercial, and ecclesiastical purposes.

Role and Responsibilities

The Property Management Department is responsible for generating reports for applications received, including:

  • New Leases
  • Renewals
  • Change of Ownership
  • Change of Use
  • Consent to Assign
  • Lease Rent Changes
  • Review of all matters in use of Land Policies

List of Properties

  • Woodbrook Estate
  • One Woodbrook Place
  • Cocorite Farm
  • Gonzales Estate
  • Parts of West Moorings
  • Specific Properties with Long Leases in Port of Spain
  • Numerous other properties owned by the Corporation

Inter-Departmental Collaboration

The Property Management Department interacts with various departments to manage its responsibilities effectively:

  • City Administration
  • Legal Advisor
  • City Assessors
  • City Engineers/Building Section
  • City Treasurer
  • Public Health Department
